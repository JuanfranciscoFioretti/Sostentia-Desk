## Welcome to Sostentia Desk!

Setting up your business management platform has never been easier. In this guide, we'll walk you through the essential steps to get started with Sostentia Desk.

### Step 1: Create Your Account

Visit our registration page and fill in your details. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

### Step 2: Set Up Your Workspace

Once logged in, you'll be guided through setting up your first workspace:

- Add your company information
- Invite team members
- Configure your preferences

### Step 3: Connect Your Mobile App

Download the Sostentia Desk mobile app from:

- **iOS**: App Store
- **Android**: Google Play Store

Use your credentials to log in and sync your data.

### Step 4: Explore Features

Take some time to explore our key features:

- Real-time dashboard
- Team collaboration tools
- Advanced analytics
- Custom reports

### Need Help?

Our support team is available 24/7 to assist you. Contact us at support@sostenatiadesk.com or visit our help center.

Happy managing!
