## Your Business, Always at Hand

The Sostentia Desk mobile app transforms how you manage your business by putting powerful tools directly in your pocket. Whether you're on the go, at the office, or anywhere in between, stay connected and productive.

## Key Features

### Reservation Management

Effortlessly manage all your reservations on the move:

- **Real-time bookings**: View and update reservations instantly
- **Client information**: Access detailed customer profiles
- **Scheduling**: Handle conflicts and reschedule with ease
- **Notifications**: Receive alerts for new bookings and changes
- **Integration**: Sync seamlessly with your desktop dashboard

### Order Management & Tracking

Keep complete control over your orders:

- **Place orders**: Create new orders on the spot
- **Real-time status**: Track order progress every step of the way
- **Order history**: Review past transactions and patterns
- **Customer details**: Access order-specific information
- **Quick actions**: Update orders or cancel when needed

### Loyalty Points System

Reward your customers and drive repeat business:

- **Points tracking**: Monitor customer reward balances
- **Redemption management**: Process point redemptions
- **Tier system**: Manage VIP and loyalty tiers
- **Automatic calculations**: Points are calculated automatically
- **Customer engagement**: Build lasting customer relationships

### Accessibility Features

We believe everyone should have access:

- **High contrast mode**: Improved visibility for vision impairments
- **Dark mode**: Reduce eye strain in low-light environments
- **Light mode**: Optimal readability in bright conditions
- **Text scaling**: Adjust font sizes to your preference
- **Voice navigation**: Control the app with voice commands
- **Screen reader support**: Full compatibility with accessibility tools

## Design Philosophy

The Sostentia Desk mobile app is built with three core themes:

- **Dark Mode**: Perfect for nighttime work and reducing fatigue
- **Light Mode**: Crystal clear in daylight with maximum readability
- **High Contrast Mode**: Enhanced visibility for users with visual impairments

Each mode switches seamlessly, positioning itself as the ultimate accessibility solution.

## Always Connected

The mobile app ensures you never miss an important update:

- Instant notifications for bookings and order changes
- Offline mode to work without internet
- Automatic sync when connectivity returns
- Cloud-backed data for security and recovery
